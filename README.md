## 👨‍💻 The Man, The Myth, The Engineer

I am a highly experienced **Full-Stack Software Engineer** with a deep specialization in **Backend Development**. I design and build scalable, robust systems that handle real-world traffic. But my curiosity doesn't stop at the cloud—I have a massive soft spot for **Embedded Systems** and hardware. 

When I'm not architecting REST APIs or optimizing database queries, you can find me bit-banging microcontrollers or reading 500-page datasheets for fun. 

- 🚀 **Core Focus:** Full-Stack Web Development, Backend Architecture, and Go/Python/TypeScript ecosystems.
- 🔬 **Secret Identity:** Hardware hacker and embedded C/C++ enthusiast. 
- ⚡ **Fun Fact:** My favorite design pattern is *"It works on my machine"* (just kidding, I use Docker).

---

## 🔬 Research & Development (A.K.A. Breaking Things to Learn)

I absolutely *love* self-driven R&D. Give me a problem without a StackOverflow answer, and I'm in my element. I thrive on researching new technologies, reverse-engineering undocumented protocols, and bridging the gap between hardware and software. 

Whether it's building a complete EMDR therapy application from scratch (UI to microcontroller) or diving deep into a new backend framework, I treat every complex problem as an R&D playground.

## 🚀 Featured Projects & R&D

*   **[Vialis](https://github.com/kalman77/vialis) & [Vialis Hardware](https://github.com/kalman77/vialis-hardware)** - A complete application ecosystem for EMDR therapy. Features a Dart/C++ UI that communicates natively with custom-programmed ESP microcontrollers. Pure R&D magic!
*   **[Gator](https://github.com/kalman77/Gator-)** - A blog aggregator built entirely in Go. Fast, concurrent, and robust.
*   **[YouTube Downloader](https://github.com/kalman77/YouTube-downloader)** - A Python-based utility to fetch and download YouTube content programmatically.
*   **[SRT Translation](https://github.com/kalman77/Srt-Translation)** - A Python tool for translating subtitle (SRT) files, bridging language barriers effortlessly.
